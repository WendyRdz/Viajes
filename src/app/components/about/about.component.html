About US

We are a team of people who love nature, outdoor activities, mountains, rivers and more.

We love to share all of our experiences touring different parts of Mexico, giving some tips and recommendations on what places to visit and what activities you can do. In this space you will find a lot of useful information for your next trip, and you will be able to share your experiences as well as sharing content with many people who visit our site, you can support us sending it in our contact section.
